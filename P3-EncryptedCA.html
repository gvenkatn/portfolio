Encrypted Chat Messenger

Overview

  • My Role: Undergraduate Student Researcher
  • Organization: National Institute of Technology Warangal
  • Completed: 10 May 2020
  • Technologies: Python, Socket Programming
The project demonstrates the successful implementation of Caesar's Cipher encryption for secure messaging. By utilizing the power of Caesar's Cipher, I have created a robust and reliable encrypted chat system that ensures the confidentiality of messages. With this project, I showcase my expertise in encryption algorithms and our commitment to promoting secure communication practices.

The Encrypted Chat Messenger allowed me to explore various methods take to ensure privacy and data protection. Through its seamless integration of Caesar's Cipher, users can exchange messages knowing that their conversations are shielded from unauthorized access. This project showcases my ability to deliver innovative solutions that prioritize security and confidentiality.
