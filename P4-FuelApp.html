Fuel App

Overview

  • My Role: Student Researcher
  • Organization: Our Own English High School Sharjah
  • Completed: 11 May 2017
  • Technologies: Python, Tkinter
The Fuel App eliminates the need for traditional cashier-based transactions, saving users valuable time and reducing wait times at the pump. The app allows users to conveniently select their desired fuel type, input the desired amount, and complete the transaction through secure and efficient payment methods.

Additionally, the Fuel App provides real-time updates on fuel prices, allowing users to make informed decisions based on current rates. With its intuitive features and user-centric design, the Fuel App revolutionizes the way customers interact with fuel stations and convenience stores. Whether it's fueling up for a road trip or grabbing a quick snack, the Fuel App ensures a smooth and hassle-free checkout experience.
